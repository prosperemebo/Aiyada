Explore Our Working Process

  • Briefing

    Meet with the client to discuss their requirements, preferences, budget, and timeline. Gather information about the project scope, goals, and any specific needs or challenges.

  • Analyzing

    Analyze the space, including its architectural features, layout, lighting, and existing furnishings. Consider factors such as building codes, regulations, and sustainability requirements.

  • Planning

    Determine the optimal layout and arrangement of furniture, fixtures, and other elements within the space. Consider factors such as traffic flow, functionality, accessibility, and zoning requirements.
